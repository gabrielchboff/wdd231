Site Plan: Porto Alegre Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

Porto Alegre Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

This website will serve as a resource hub for businesses in the city of Porto Alegre. It will provide information on local events, host networking opportunities, create business directories, and encourage local shopping. The site aims to promote local economic growth, support local businesses, and foster a sense of community among businesses.

Target Market

Business owners and patrons in the city of Porto Alegre and surrounding areas.

Site Goals

  • Improve member engagement by providing valuable resources and networking opportunities.
  • Attract new businesses and encourage economic development in the community.
  • Improve the visibility and reputation of the chamber as a trusted authority in local business matters.

User Personas

Small Business Owner: Maria, 35, owns a boutique shop. Seeks networking, business resources, and marketing support.
Corporate Executive: Diego, 47, executive at a large company. Interested in partnerships, economic development, and networking.
New Resident: Francisco, recently moved to Porto Alegre. Wants to learn about local services, business directories, events, and community opportunities.

Scenarios

  • A business owner visits the site to find membership benefits, fees, and application info.
  • A community member browses the events calendar and registers for activities.
  • A visitor explores the site for information on local businesses, workforce, and quality of life.

Overview

The website will apply course concepts to create a responsive, standards-based chamber of commerce site for Porto Alegre. The site will promote business interests, foster networking, and advocate for the community.

What is a Chamber of Commerce?

A chamber of commerce is a local organization of businesses working together to strengthen business interests, foster networking, and advocate for the community.

Page Specifications

  • Home: Attention-grabbing info, community news, weather, calls to action, business spotlights.
  • Discover: Local history, demographics, current events, image montage of the area.
  • Directory: List of local businesses and organizations.
  • Join: Membership benefits, application form, three membership levels (non-profit, silver, gold) with different perks.

Developmental Specifications

  • Valid, semantic, and contemporary HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Consistent design and navigation across all pages.
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop.
  • Meets all functional, content, and behavior specifications.
  • Positive user experience with standard design principles and easy-to-use interfaces.
  • Published site meets course learning outcomes.
